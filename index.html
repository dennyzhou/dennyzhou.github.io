DENNY ZHOU

Principal Scientist/Research Director
 Google Brain
Email: dennyzhou at google dot com


Founder and lead of the Reasoning Team in Google Brain. Solve Artificial General Intelligence (AGI) by building & teaching large language models (LLMs) to achieve human-level reasoning. Our research breakthroughs include: instruction tuning (FLAN2), chain-of-thought prompting, self-consistency decoding, least-to-most prompting, and emergent properties of LLMs. Google CEO Sundar Pichai presented our work in Google I/O 2022.
